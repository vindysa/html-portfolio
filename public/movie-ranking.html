The Best Movies According to Vallen

My top 3 personal favorite movies of all time.

Avatar

Easily my favorite movie. It was my first real exposure to animation of that caliber, and the visuals alone were stunningly beautiful. It set a new starndard for that genre of cinema.

Ford vs. Ferrari

Automobiles are near and dear to my heart. Seeing the battle between these two coporations, and the struggles of two men to pioneer and develop was so fascinating to watch.

Inception

I won't lie, I had to watch this movie more than once to figure out how many dreams within the dream they were going. I enjoyed having to think about what I was watching for a change over just consuming the entertainment in front of me.
