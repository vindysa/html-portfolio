About Me

I didn't fully understand how it worked, but I realized then that a small amount of automation effort could save a lot of man hours if utilized on a larger scale.

While I still have a great deal to learn, it is the broad application of programming that excites me. My entire life, I have always enjoyed learning new things and exporing new hobbies. That is part of what I find so thrilling about programming. There is always something new to learn. There are always emerging techniques and framworks that allows you to code in new and exciting ways, and allows avenues that help deligate more tedious tasks to machines -- thus saving people valuble time both in business or even in their personal lives.
